About This Project

FoodPriceAI predicts food prices in Nigerian markets using advanced AI trained on historical vendor and product data, providing users with accurate and practical price estimates.


Market Data

Uses vendor info, product categories, and packaging sizes to understand market price trends.

AI Training

Data transformations normalize prices, helping the AI model identify patterns effectively.

Prediction Output

Predicted prices are displayed in Naira, giving clear and actionable estimates for users.


Nithub AI Ecosystem

Part of the Nithub AI Ecosystem, this project delivers real-world AI solutions that provide economic insights for Nigerian markets.
